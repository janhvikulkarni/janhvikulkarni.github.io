    heelscation

    Collaborators: Brianna Li, Jackson Lei, Kelly Yu

    Heelscation is designed to be a dream vacation planner. This was my final project for COMP 426 at UNC. Users can:

    • Create an account
    • Take a personality quiz to receive a potential vacation spot based on their preferences
    • Record personal notes to plan their vacation
    • Access a weather forecast for any destination
    • Add other users of the app as friends
